About

Hi there! My name is Avery Lessard, I am a tattoo artist born and raised in Windsor, Ontario. I started my tattooing journey in 2018 and became a full-time artist in 2020. I graduated from the University of Windsor in 2020 with a Bachelor of Fine Arts and a focus on drawing and painting.

I love tattooing in a variety of styles but I mostly practice illustrative black and grey with American Traditional inspiration. I also love tattooing with colour and fine line styles. I am always trying to push my work creatively and try new techniques when given the opportunity.

My favourite thing about tattooing is that it allows me to help people tell their story and feel confident in their skin. My goal is to collaborate with my clients to create meaningful and unique tattoos that withstand the test of time. It is important to me to do work that I am proud of and that my client is proud to wear for the rest of their life.

Thank you for reading!
